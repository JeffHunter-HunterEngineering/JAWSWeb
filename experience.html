Office Building - Scottsdale, Arizona

This project consisted of a two-story office building in Scottsdale, Arizona. This project was unique because it was an infill site which was originally designed as a second phase of development within an existing parking lot. This was a ground-up facility currently under management by our firm, currently the building is a single tenant, long term veterinary hospital. Industrial Building - Mesa, Arizona

This project is a large rear loaded industrial facility in Mesa, Arizona. This project is unique because of the access condition. The Town of Queen Creek owns the frontage road and a private developer owns the west access drive so access was tough to obtain for the project. Construction is anticipated in the second quarter 2024. Scope includes Site selection, Due-diligence, Planning, Engineering, Construction, and Lease up.

Ground Lease - Phoenix, Arizona

This was originally purchased for development and then the recession hit. Because of the turbulent market at the time, we signed a development agreement and ground lease with Enterprise for a truck rental facility in Phoenix, Arizona. The project was fully improvement and currently has a long-term lease. The project is unique because it was purchased for development and ended up becoming a ground lease. Scope includes Site selection, Due-diligence, Planning, Rezoning, Engineering and Property Management.

Storage Facility - Surprise, Arizona

This project consisted of a large multi-story self-storage facility in Surprise, Arizona. This project was unique because it won the Green Storage Facility of the Year award by Self Storage Magazine. This was a ground-up facility and was managed by our firm and then ultimately sold to Public Storage after 93% stabilization. Scope: Site selection, Due-diligence, Planning, Engineering, Construction, Lease up and Property Management.

Industrial Building - Goodyear, Arizona

Redevelopment Office Building - Scottsdale, Arizona

This was a redevelopment project of a large two-story office building in Scottsdale, Arizona. Honor Health Medical Office Building - Peoria, Arizona

This project consisted of a single-story medical office building in Peoria, Arizona. This project was unique because it was an infill site but in an area of need for medical office.
